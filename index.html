Art Of Coding

Programs must be written for people to read, and only incidentally for machines to execute. That's what exactly the app is about.

Responsive Design

Seamlessly designed to collaborate codes of different languages, even in offline mode (once you have opened the program, online).

Feature Reach

It is just like facebook for code lovers, where you can post your favourite codes, to express or to impress. Only Android users can use it.

Useful Documentation

The codes are available in seamlees documented form, from where one can copy the codes. The posts can also be made in documented format (only .txt files are acceptable).

Time Saving

You can find your usefull, favourite codes in the bookmark section, if you have bookmarked the programs previously. You don't have to waste time in searching for it among all.

Feedback

There is a feedback section along the navigation section, where you can camplain for nay problem regarding the app, or can give your views on it.
